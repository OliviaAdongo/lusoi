Our Services

Our comprehensive range of services caters to people of all levels.

Recreational Facilities

At Loisoi, we believe in offering a holistic experience that goes beyond just accommodations. Guests can enjoy a variety of recreational facilities:
  • Swimming Pool: Our family-friendly pool is designed for both relaxation and fun. With a dedicated children’s area, it’s the perfect place for guests of all ages to unwind.
  • Nature Walks & Hiking: Loisoi’s expansive ranch offers ample opportunity for exploration. Take a scenic walk or embark on a hiking trail to discover Nyeri’s rich landscapes.
  • Event Spaces: Our lush gardens are ideal for weddings, family gatherings, and corporate events. Whether you’re planning an intimate celebration or a larger event, our beautiful outdoor spaces and professional event planning services ensure a seamless experience.

Business & Event Facilities

Lusoi Ranch and Resort offers the perfect setting for your business or special event. Our serene, nature-inspired venue provides a professional yet tranquil environment to make any occasion memorable:
  • Conference Facilities: Our conference room is equipped to accommodate up to 30 guests, making it the perfect space for corporate meetings, intimate conferences, or training sessions. Overlooking a tranquil dam, it provides a peaceful yet professional setting for productive gatherings.
  • Team Building Activities: Strengthen your team’s connections with our tailor-made team-building programs. These activities range from physical challenges to mental problem-solving exercises, designed to promote creativity, trust, and collaboration.

Dining Experiences

Food is at the heart of Loisoi’s experience, and we take pride in offering culinary delights that celebrate Kenya’s diverse flavors:
  • Ndovu Bar: Named after the elephant, the Ndovu Bar offers a cozy, rustic atmosphere where guests can enjoy drinks by the fire after a day of exploring. It’s the perfect spot to relax and unwind.
  • Mount Kenya Bar: Inspired by the towering mountain, this serene bar offers a variety of cocktails, wines, and spirits. Whether you're celebrating with friends or simply enjoying a quiet evening, the Mount Kenya Bar provides a relaxed and inviting setting.

Packages

We offer tailored packages to suit a variety of needs, ensuring every guest enjoys a personalized experience:
  • Accommodation Packages: Whether you're planning a romantic weekend, a family vacation, or a corporate retreat, our tailored accommodation packages ensure a seamless, all-inclusive experience.
  • Wedding & Celebration Packages: Make your dream wedding a reality with Lusoi’s wedding packages, set against the stunning backdrop of our landscaped gardens. Our dedicated team will handle everything from planning to execution, so you can focus on enjoying your special day.
