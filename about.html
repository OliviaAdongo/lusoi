About Us

At Loisoi Ranch and Resort, we are inspired by Kenya’s rich heritage and natural beauty.

Our Story

Loisoi Ranch and Resort was created out of a deep passion for Kenya’s history and natural beauty. Inspired by the union of the ancient and modern worlds, our founder envisioned a space where guests can reflect on the origins of human civilization while enjoying the comforts of contemporary life.

Nestled on a sprawling ranch, Loisoi combines eco-friendly practices with modern luxuries to provide a harmonious retreat. Every detail of the resort, from our sustainable construction materials to our community-driven initiatives, reflects our dedication to protecting the environment and uplifting local communities.

Contact Us

At Loisoi, each element tells a story. Our cave-inspired cottages remind us of humanity’s beginnings, while their chic, modern interiors represent progress and innovation. Our Ndovu Bar, named after the mighty elephant, celebrates human resilience, and the Mount Kenya Bar pays homage to one of Kenya’s most iconic landmarks. With every meal, room, and experience, Loisoi offers a unique tribute to Kenya’s heritage and a bridge to its future.

Our Vision

From sleek modernism to timeless elegance, we infuse your experience with a touch of our artistic ingenuity.
